TOP 15 PLACES TO VISIT IN THE PHILIPPINES

The Pear of the Orient and one of the most visited country in Asia, people who has visited are in awe of all the best places to visit in the Philippines! Check out this post to learn more about it.

The Philippines as a country is graced by beautiful beaches, lovely weather and excellent opportunities for island hopping, diving, and surfing. For years this small yet surprisingly famous country has attracted a decent stream of travelers from across the world. However, the state offers you a lot more than just snorkeling and sand with many other tourist attractions in the Philippines.
Besides the coastlines and beaches, there are plenty of places to visit in the Philippines including mystical villages, smothering jungle peaks, ancient rice and paddy fields, and highlights of Spanish churches. On a more closer look, you will find the island to have a vibrant mix of cultures like American, Malay, Islamic and Spanish in an array of celebrations, dazzling colonial towns and tantalizing food with more resemblance to Latin America than Asia.

Filipinos welcome visitors with colorful fiestas held throughout the year based on the Roman Catholic calendar with food heavily influenced by native and Spanish traditions. With more than 7000 en-thralling island beauties to choose from, if you have been planning your first or n-th Philippines travel, get ready for idyllic hills of Batanes, breathtaking cascaded of Cebu Lake and much more.

Many awestruck famous Philippines destinations are worthy to be included in your travel bucket list. Whether you love the sun-kissed lazy beaches or historic sites, you will get a complete sense of wanderlust in the Philippines. There are famous cities in the Philippines like Cebu, Manila, etc. where travelers can learn more about the development, mixed cultures or can find out about other Philippines points of interest. To make your visit less confusing on selecting where to go on this tropical hub, here’s the list of some of the most remarkable sightseeing places to inspire you with a lovely start to your Filipino adventure.
